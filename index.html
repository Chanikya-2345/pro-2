The Magic Behind Layali Lumina

"We don't create candles. We create moments."

What "Layali Lumina" Promise you

At Layali Lumina, we believe the most beautiful moments happen after sunset. Every candle is handcrafted in small batches using premium ingredients, capturing the warmth, mystery and beauty of magical nights.

Every element matters. We source exclusively 100% natural soy wax from sustainable farms, blend our fragrances from premium essential oils and fragrance concentrates, and hand-pour each candle in meticulously controlled small batches. Our reusable, recyclable vessels are selected for both aesthetic appeal and environmental integrity.

We believe that true luxury is inherently sustainable. Our carbon-neutral operations, ethical supply chain, and commitment to zero-waste practices ensure that indulging in beautiful ambiance never compromises our planet's future.
